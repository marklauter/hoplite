# Front-load important words

The most informative word goes first in the sentence and first in the bullet. The reader who scans only the openings still gets the most.

## How to apply

- In a sentence, lead with the subject or the most informative noun.
- In a bullet, lead with the principle name or the imperative verb; the descriptor follows.
- In a heading, lead with what the section is *about*, not the meta ("Database configuration" beats "About database configuration").
