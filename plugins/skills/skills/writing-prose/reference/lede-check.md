# Lede check

Read the first paragraph alone. Does it name what the artifact is for? Could a reader stop there and still take away the right impression?

## How to apply

1. Isolate the first paragraph from the rest of the document.
2. Ask: what is this artifact for? Is the answer in the paragraph?
3. Ask: what does the reader gain from reading on? Is the value proposition there?
4. If both answers are "no," rewrite the opening.

## What a good lede does

- Names the artifact's intent in plain language.
- Sets the audience and the takeaway.
- Stands alone — works as a one-paragraph summary for a reader who reads no further.
