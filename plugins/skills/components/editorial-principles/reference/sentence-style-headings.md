# Sentence-style headings

First word and proper nouns only capitalized. No title case.

## How to apply

- Good: `## Forms are the schema`, `## Self-review heuristics`.
- Bad: `## Forms Are The Schema`, `## Self-Review Heuristics`.
- Proper nouns capitalize per their own rules: `## Using the Reviewer skill`.

## Why

Title case is heavyweight visual noise. Sentence-style reads like prose — the reader's eye doesn't have to parse the capitalization pattern. It's also one fewer thing to be inconsistent about across artifacts.
