The connected subgraph a [[walk]] returns: the seed resources plus the resources and relationships reachable within its depth.

## Notes

Unlike a flat result set, a neighborhood carries topology — the relationships traversed and the paths to each resource. Depth zero is the degenerate case: the seeds alone, the same shape a [[match]] returns.
