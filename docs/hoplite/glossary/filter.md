A crisp condition: it tests a resource or statement for membership — a tag, a claim, a relationship — in or out, with no score.

## Examples

- `tags contains "todo"` — set membership over a label.
- `status = "draft"` — a claim value.
- `cites` — a relationship condition for a [[walk]], named by its label.
