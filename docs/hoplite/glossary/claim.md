A feature advanced as true by the author or Hoplite.

## Examples

- A `tags: [hoplite, glossary]` line the author wrote — advanced as true, and revisable.
- An edge Hoplite derived between two documents and asserted into the graph.

## Contrasts

- `fact` — a claim is advanced as true and so can be contested; a fact is intrinsic, true by the document's own existence.
