An action possibility a [[glossary/feature]] offers the agent.

## Examples

- the vocabulary affords [[glossary/survey]] — discovering what it holds before a predicate is composed.
- a property affords filtering, ordering, and ranking the corpus by a value.
- a stereotype affords filtering edges by what a link means, not just that it exists.
